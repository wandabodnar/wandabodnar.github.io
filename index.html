Wanda Bodnar

Marine Geospatial Data Scientist & SUP Coach

I am a marine geospatial data scientist applying spatial data in the context of aquatic habitat conservation, restoration and climate resilience within the Greater Thames Estuary (UK). I also lead natural history and ecology-focused stand up paddleboarding (SUP) sessions on the upper Thames Estuary.

My interests include marine and estuarine science, climate science, cartography, data visualisation, science communication and aquatic outdoor activities, especially for the purposes of improving mental health and wellbeing.
